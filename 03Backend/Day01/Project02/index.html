Step 2 of 3 — permanent, but local
What changed: tasks now save to this browser's localStorage, so they survive a refresh — even survive closing the tab and coming back. Try it: add tasks, refresh, watch them stay.
The catch to feel next: this storage belongs to this one browser on this one machine. Open the same file on your phone, another laptop, or even a different browser here — you'll start empty. The data has no shared home. That's the wall a backend + database breaks through (Step 3).
